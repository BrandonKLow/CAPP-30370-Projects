Article

Mark Sanchez says he, not Geno Smith, won NY Jets' QB Competition

by Seth Walder, September 12, 2012

FOXBOROUGH - Mark Sanchez, who watched Thursday night's game with an injured right shoulder, believes that he won the Jets' quarterback competition in the preseason over Geno Smith.

"I won the competition," the fifth-year quarterback told NFL Network. "There's no doubt."

When Sanchez was asked if he has a tear in his shoulder, the quarterback said, "I really can't get into that part."

Entering the second game of the regular season, the Jets still have never officially answered the question of who had won the quarterback job. Instead, they described Sanchez as "day-to-day" for two-and-a-half weeks and started Smith in each of their first two games without committing to him for longer than that.
